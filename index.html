What is GuessIT?

GuessIT is a simple and fun desktop game featuring three mini-challenges designed to test your memory, logic, and quick thinking.

Guess The Password

Try to figure out a secret 5-character password composed of letters and numbers. You have 15 chances to guess it, with helpful feedback after each attempt showing which characters are correct or misplaced.

Guess The Color

A mystery color appears on screen, and your task is to guess its RGB values. Each guess tells you whether your red, green, and blue values need to be higher or lower. Use the feedback to zero in on the exact shade.

Click The Target

A fast-paced reflex game where the goal is to click a moving or disappearing target. It's still in its early stage, giving room to grow with more challenge or scoring elements.
